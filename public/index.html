Chant Digger (Max Haas)

Restored version from http://www.oralhistoryofchant.ch/chantdigger via WayBackMachine. May be broken. Created by Max Haas and published with a vague license statement: "Code und Daten sind im Sinne eines open source-Unternehmens frei: Ich bitte, eventuelle Eigenprodukte neu zu benennen, damit mir der Name ChantDigger bleibt". You will find more information in the Guided Tour and Kurzanleitung - Tim Eipert, September 2021 (Github - Uni Würzburg)
Now you have the ability to load the original data transcribed from Max Haas as well as the editions of Corpus Monodicum (CM). This is just a experimental version and there might be some issues. Tim Eipert - 05.06.2024.
